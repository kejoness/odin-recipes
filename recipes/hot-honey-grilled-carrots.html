Hot Honey Grilled Carrots

Description

The sauce on these hot honey grilled carrots has a great balance of flavors that compliment the charred sweet carrots. It's a simple yet beautiful side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound carrots, peeled, cut in 4-inch lengths, then halved or quartered if large
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang or harissa paste
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch crushe red pepper (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Brush carrots with oil and grill, covered, over direct heat until fork tender and lightly charred, 14 to 16 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile whisk together honey, gochujang, butter, lime juice, salt, and crushed red pepper, if using, in a small bowl.
  4. Brush cooked carrots with honey mixture, and grill 1 minute more.
  5. Transfer carrots to a serving platter. Drizzle with remaining honey mixture.
  6. Sprinkle with cilantro to serve.

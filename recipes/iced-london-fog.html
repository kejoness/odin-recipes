Iced London Fog

Description

This iced London fog is a creamy Earl Grey tea iced latte with a hint of vanilla, as refreshing on a hot day as a cool mist.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 Earl Grey tea bag
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla syrup
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil; remove from heat and add tea bag. Steep for 5 minutes.
  2. Fill a tall glass with ice; pour tea over ice. Pour in milk and add vanilla syrup. Stir.
